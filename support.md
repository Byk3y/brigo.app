## üß¨ The Brigo Methodology: The Science of Learning

Brigo isn't just an app; it's a scientific study tool built on two pillars of educational psychology:

- **Active Recall:** Instead of passively re-reading your notes (which is often ineffective), Brigo‚Äôs quizzes and flashcards force your brain to retrieve information. Research shows that **Active Recall is up to 3x more effective** than traditional reviewing.
- **Spaced Repetition:** Our Study Pet and streak mechanics are designed to bring you back to your materials at the optimal intervals. By reviewing content just as you are about to forget it, you move information from short-term memory to long-term mastery.

### üß™ Troubleshooting "Bad" AI Outputs
Because Brigo uses Large Language Models (LLMs), there is a small chance the AI may "hallucinate" or provide a prediction that feels slightly off.

**How to get better results:**
- **Provide More Context:** If a prediction is vague, try uploading more specific materials (e.g., your actual past exam papers instead of just general notes).
- **Clear & Legible Uploads:** If your handwritten notes are blurry, the AI might misinterpret key terms. Ensure high-contrast, well-lit photos.
- **Re-Generate:** You can re-run a generation with different materials in the Studio to refine the AI's focus.

## üí≥ Billing & Subscriptions (Self-Service)

Most billing inquiries can be resolved directly through your device settings. Since Brigo uses the Apple App Store and Google Play Store for all transactions, we do not have direct access to your credit card information or the ability to cancel subscriptions on your behalf.

### How to Cancel or Manage your Subscription

#### **On iPhone/iPad (iOS):**
1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone.
2. Tap your **Name** at the very top.
3. Tap **Subscriptions**.
4. Find **Brigo** in the list and tap it.
5. Tap **Cancel Subscription**. (If you don't see "Cancel," the subscription is already canceled and won't renew).

#### **On Android:**
1. Open the **Google Play Store** app.
2. Tap your **Profile Icon** at the top right.
3. Tap **Payments & Subscriptions** > **Subscriptions**.
4. Select **Brigo** from the list.
5. Tap **Cancel Subscription** and follow the instructions.

### Refunds
Refunds are handled entirely by Apple and Google. We are unable to issue refunds directly.
- **Apple Users:** Visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com) to request a refund.
- **Android Users:** You can request a refund through the [Google Play website](https://support.google.com/googleplay/workflow/9813544).

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is Brigo's prediction a guarantee of my exam results?**  
**A:** No. Brigo is a powerful study aid designed to highlight high-probability topics based on your specific materials. It should be used to *prioritize* your revision, not as a replacement for studying the full curriculum.

**Q: My AI generation is taking a long time. Why?**  
**A:** "Deep Extraction" and "Exam Prediction" are computationally heavy. Most tasks finish in under 60 seconds, but complex PDFs or long YouTube videos may take up to 2 minutes.

**Q: Can I share my notebooks with friends?**  
**A:** Currently, notebooks are private to your account to ensure data security and personalized AI analysis.
